Our Mission

Purchasing tickets through Internet is another convenient way to travel with Bangladesh Railway. Register yourself with your cell phone number, book online and your ticket details will then be emailed to you instantly after payment through your VISA/MASTER/DBBL Nexus cards. Print it out and bring it along with the registered cellphone number and the card you used to purchase the ticket to the station at least 15 minutes prior to your journey. There are specially designated counters in the online stations where etickets can be printed and delivered to you.
